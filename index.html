The Urgency

Kenya's Hidden Food Safety Crisis

Aflatoxin contamination threatens food safety, public health, and agricultural trade across Kenya.

Public Health Hazard

Over 4 million Kenyans at risk of chronic aflatoxin exposure, linked to liver cancer and immune suppression.

Market Rejection

Up to 45% of maize lots rejected, causing 50M USD annual losses for farmers and traders.

Post-Harvest Losses

Traditional drying methods promote fungal growth, leading to significant post-harvest losses.
